üî¥ Common Port Error Types:

  • Port Down/Link Down: Physical port is not operational or cable disconnected
  • CRC Errors: Cyclic Redundancy Check errors indicating data corruption
  • Link Flapping: Port going up and down repeatedly
  • Speed/Duplex Mismatch: Incorrect negotiation between devices
  • Collision Errors: Multiple devices transmitting simultaneously
  • Packet Loss: Dropped packets due to congestion or errors
  • Input/Output Errors: Problems receiving or transmitting data
  • Bandwidth Saturation: Port operating at maximum capacity
  • SFP/Transceiver Errors: Issues with fiber optic modules
